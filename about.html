Dr. Giriraj Mandal(BDS, MDS)

Prosthodontist and Implantologist

Dr. Giriraj Mandal, MDS, is a highly regarded Prosthodontist and Implantologist based in Kolkata, India. He operates a leading Dental and Implant Clinic, specializing in prosthodontics and dental implantology.With a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) degree in Prosthodontics, Dr. Mandal excels in crafting customized dental prosthetics, including crowns, bridges, dentures, and dental implants. His expertise ensures optimal functionality and aesthetics for every patient. Dr. Mandal is particularly skilled in dental implantology, offering long-lasting, natural-looking solutions for tooth replacement. His precise implant placements result in successful outcomes and improved quality of life for patients. At his modern clinic, Dr. Mandal utilizes cutting-edge dental technologies, creating a comfortable environment for patients. He is committed to ongoing professional development, staying updated with the latest advancements in dentistry. His dedication to excellence has established him as a trusted dental specialist in Kolkata, providing top-tier prosthodontic and dental implant services to the community.
